Usage guide

Welcome to the Weather Application! This guide will help you navigate and make the most out of the features provided to access weather information for any city.

1. City: Use the search bar to enter the desired city.

2. Weather Information: Get the current temperature, minimum and maximum temperature, humidity, and more.

3. Detailed Info: Click for additional weather details for the selected city.

4. Other Cities: Compare weather conditions for different cities.

5. Navigation: Use the top bar to access different sections.


Enjoy using the Weather Application!
